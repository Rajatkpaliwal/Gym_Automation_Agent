FitLife Gym — Membership Plans and Pricing

We offer three flexible membership plans to suit every lifestyle and budget.

The Basic Plan costs ₹1,499 per month and includes unlimited access to the gym floor, locker room facilities, and two complimentary group classes per week. This plan is ideal for members who prefer independent workouts.

The Premium Plan costs ₹2,499 per month and includes everything in the Basic Plan plus unlimited group classes, one personal training session per month, access to the sauna and steam room, and priority booking for all classes.

The Annual Elite Plan costs ₹19,999 per year (equivalent to ₹1,667 per month) and includes everything in Premium, three personal training sessions per month, free guest passes (2 per month), nutrition consultation, and a complimentary FitLife gym bag.

All plans include a free first-time assessment with a certified trainer. There are no joining fees for the first month if you sign up online. Students and senior citizens receive a 15% discount on all plans with valid ID.

FitLife Gym — Trial Class and Joining Process

Prospective members can book a free trial class to experience FitLife before committing to a membership. The trial includes access to any one group class of their choice plus a 30-minute floor induction with a trainer.

To book a trial, simply provide your name, preferred date, and preferred time during this conversation. Our team will confirm your slot via WhatsApp.

To join as a full member after your trial, visit either branch with a government-issued photo ID and one passport-sized photograph. Payment can be made by cash, UPI, or card.

Existing members can refer friends and earn one free month added to their membership for every successful referral.

FitLife Gym — Policies

Dress code: Proper athletic wear and clean, non-marking sports shoes are mandatory on the gym floor.

Code of conduct: Members are expected to re-rack weights, wipe equipment after use, and respect other members' space and time.

Personal training: PT sessions are by appointment only. Cancellations with less than 4 hours' notice will be charged in full.

Membership freeze: Members can freeze their membership for up to 60 days per year with 7 days' prior notice. A nominal fee of ₹199 per month applies during freeze.

Membership cancellation: 30 days' written notice is required. No refund is provided for the remaining period on monthly plans. Annual plans may receive a pro-rata refund minus a ₹999 administrative fee.

Lost and found: Items found in the gym are held for 7 days at reception. FitLife is not responsible for loss of personal belongings.
